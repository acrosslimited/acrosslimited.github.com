Description

Hurry up! The evil pigs are coming! It's time to create your own barriers to protect your mushrooms! Their lives are in your hands!

STORY

Once upon a time, there was a happy pig family. One day, something unknown dropped from nowhere whilst the piggies were sleeping soundly. Ka-BOMB! The piggies were attacked by the unknown. They were thrown to a forest where a group of cutie mushrooms stay. The pigs felt frustrated with the attack and they feel envy against the happy mushrooms; the pigs became evil and decided to destroy the peaceful environment in the forest. The innocent mushrooms feel helpless and they wish someone could save them. Now, you are the only one to save their lives!
